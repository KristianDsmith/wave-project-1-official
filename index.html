About

Welcome to Wave surf, yoga, and sustainable retreat.

An exciting surf and yoga retreat created by Sarah and Daniel a home away from home. Where we welcome all into our community of mindfulness, surfing, and yoga. Set in the idyllic surroundings of Portuguese tranquility. A surfer’s paradise in the open water or meditating and starting your day with yoga in the sun and fresh slaty air. Enjoy our sustainable restaurant eating together as a community. Enjoying the chef delights sourced locally or grown on site. What an away to bring balance and perspective to your life.

Welcome to our community WAVE!

Daniel Smith

Chef / Surf Instructor

Energetic creative culinary master, passion for food, surfing and yoga. Daniel has over 20 years’ experience in the restaurant and hospitality business. Working all over the world from Ibiza, London, Sweden, and Thailand to name a few. Now finally Daniel gets to live out his dream and welcome guest into the Wave community. You will be able to enjoy watching him prepare the wave meals and ask questions or join in. Also expect a fun approach to his surfing sessions.

Sarah Smith

Yoga Teacher / Surf instructor

Playful and embodied yoga instructor with a passion for surfing, writing and spiritualty. Sarah has over 15 years of experience in yoga and surfing. Having gained her experience form her travels in South America and Australia. Now Sarah lives the dream of sharing her knowledge in meditation and yoga. While living close to nature and the water where she can enjoy the days surf. In the evenings you can catch Sarah reciting her poems of healing or playing guitar by the open fire.
